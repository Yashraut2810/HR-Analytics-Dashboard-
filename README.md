HR Analytics Dashboard
**Overview**
The HR Analytics Dashboard is an interactive data analysis project designed to help organizations understand workforce trends, improve employee retention, and make data-driven HR decisions.
It provides insights into key HR metrics such as employee attrition, demographics, job roles, and performance.

**Objectives**
Analyze employee data to identify attrition patterns
Track key HR KPIs
Provide actionable insights for better decision-making
Visualize workforce trends in an intuitive dashboard

**key Features**

Attrition analysis (who is leaving & why)
Employee demographics (age, gender, department)
Job role & salary insights
KPI tracking (attrition rate, average income, tenure)
Interactive filters & slicers

 **Tech Stack**
Power BI – Dashboard & visualization
Excel – Data cleaning & preprocessing
DAX – Calculated measures & KPIs

**Dataset**
The dataset includes:
Employee demographics
Job roles & departments
Salary details
Attrition status

 **Key Insights**
Higher attrition observed in specific job roles
Employees with lower tenure are more likely to leave
Salary and job satisfaction impact retention
Certain departments show higher turnover rates
